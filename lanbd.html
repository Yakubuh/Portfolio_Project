About

This project was inspired by my passion for both software engineering and physics. It represents my journey of balancing these two diverse fields and exploring the synergy between science and technology.

As a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, this platform is designed to empower individuals to create impressive online portfolios and share their talents with the world.
